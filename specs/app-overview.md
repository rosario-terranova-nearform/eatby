# App Overview: EatBy

**"Because your fridge shouldn’t be a science experiment."**

### 📋 The Concept

**EatBy** is a minimalist, high-utility mobile application designed to eliminate food waste through aggressive organization and proactive reminders. Unlike complex inventory apps, **EatBy** focuses on one thing: the ticking clock. By combining a familiar calendar interface with lightning-fast barcode scanning, it helps users track exactly when their food expires and ensures they get a "nudge" before it’s too late.

### 🛠️ How It Works

1. **Scan:** Point your camera at any food barcode to instantly identify the item.
2. **Set:** Manually toggle the "Deadline" date (Best Before/Use By).
3. **Visualize:** The item is instantly plotted onto a Google Calendar-style interface.
4. **Save:** Receive a push notification exactly 24 hours before the deadline.

---

### ✨ Core Features

- **The "Pantry Calendar":** Visualizes your groceries as time-blocks. User will see a **Month View**, and he can see exactly which "food deadlines" are approaching at a glance.
- **Rapid Entry System:** A streamlined "Scan" screen that opens a modal scanner. It’s designed for speed—perfect for logging a full bag of groceries in under a minute.
- **Privacy-First (Local Storage):** **EatBy** stores all data locally on the user's device. No accounts, no cloud syncing, and no data-sharing. Your grocery habits stay yours.
- **Smart Reminders:** The app monitors your local database and triggers a notification the day before any deadline, giving you enough time to plan a meal around that specific ingredient.
- **Essential Editing:** Total control over your data. Easily rename items (e.g., changing "Generic Milk" to "The Good Milk"), update deadlines, or delete items once they’ve been eaten.

---

### 🎯 Who Is It For?

- **The Eco-Conscious:** People looking to reduce their environmental footprint by hitting "Zero Waste."
- **The Budget Hunter:** Households tired of literally throwing money in the trash when expensive perishables spoil.
- **The Minimalist:** Users who want a tool that does one job perfectly without the bloat of social features or ads.

---

### 🚀 The Value Proposition

**EatBy** turns the chore of inventory management into a visual habit. By treating food expiry like a scheduled appointment, it shifts the user's mindset from _"Oh, I forgot I had that"_ to _"I have a deadline to meet tonight."_ **It’s not just an inventory; it’s a plan for your next meal.**
